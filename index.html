Industrial abseiling

Abseiling rope access is the best way to perform work at heights.
We can go where others can't, providing safe and effective solutions for your difficult to access jobs.

We provide immediately available qualified efficient abseilers with higher engineering education and extensive experience to carry out a wide variety of industrial high rope access jobs which are fully trained to undertake any project that you need doing. Our industrial abseiling rope access staff work in myriad of industry sectors in difficult to access areas, at any constructions and harsh environments, such as confined spaces and high level access structures.

Abseiling rope access is less disruptive and more cost effective than using scaffolding, monkey board, cherry pickers/telehandler, crane or truck mounted boom lifts. Rope access equipment is lightweight, highly mobile, and can be used to quickly implement the most secure access to any point of work without the risk of causing disruption to contruction, staff or residents inside the building.

No matter what it is you require, you are guaranteed a team with the right experience to ensure the best results. We take up any project no matter how high or complex it is. From window and cladding cleaning to building maintenance, cabling and wiring, painting, banner installation, we are the ones to call. Whatever projects you have, we are here to do them quickly, safely and efficiently.

We can work around the times you require and assess any potential projects. Take advantage of the commercial benefits of rope access abseiling including its convenience, ease of use and unobtrusive nature for all your high level cleaning and maintenance requirements.

Safety is our priority! Remaining safe while working at heights is our priority, and we take the necessary steps to ensure the safety not only of our team but other staff who are on site. All our abseiling technicians are qualified and fully insured. We are very strict on procedures and checks are conducted on a regular basis. Rescue procedures are included in every task we perform.
